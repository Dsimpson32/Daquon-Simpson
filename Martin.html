About The Show

The 90s sitcom set in Detroit follows Martin Lawerence in the role of Martin Payne,
a disc jockey who later becomes a talk show host with a girlfriend name Gina Waters (Tisha Campbell-Martin).
Episodes center Martin's inconsiderate behaviors and smart mouth towards his friends, neighbors,
and whoever else finds themselves in his presence. The show aired on Fox from August 27, 1992 to May 1, 1997
after five successful seasons.

Along with the main character Martin Payne Martin Lawerence played many of the show's recurring characters.
One of which is a loudmouthed, aging, once-flashy but faded Detroit pimp.
Voted Detroit's "Player of the Year" He often speaks in rhyming sentences. Usually appears saying his signature
phrase "Oooh, oooh, oo-oo-ooh, I say Jerome's in the
house, I say Jerome's in the ah-um-ah-um...watch yo' mouth!"
